Performance Case Study

Sales & Regional Performance Analytics

Client: Choco de Luxe  |  Industry: Premium Food & Beverage  |  Tool: Tableau

Tip: Swipe/trackpad left-right or click the dots to view the next dashboard.

Problem Statement

Despite strong brand equity and product quality, Choco de Luxe lacked a centralized, data-driven view of sales and operational performance across Europe.

Key challenges included:

  • Underperforming cities and regions
  • Inconsistent delivery outcomes (completed vs cancelled orders)
  • Limited visibility into channel profitability and salesperson productivity
  • Difficulty aligning product strategy with evolving customer behaviour

Leadership required actionable insights to optimise sales strategy, delivery efficiency, and regional growth.

Project Objective

Analyse Choco de Luxe’s sales performance across locations, channels, salespeople, and products, and deliver interactive Tableau dashboards to support strategic decision-making on:

  • Product positioning
  • Channel effectiveness
  • Sales force productivity
  • Regional expansion and optimization

Introduction

Today, I’ll be walking you through an interactive Tableau dashboard built for Choco de Luxe, a premium artisanal chocolate brand operating across multiple European cities. The purpose of this dashboard is to provide a unified view of sales performance, regional trends, channel profitability, product margins, and salesperson productivity—supporting data-driven strategic decisions.

Executive Sales Performance Overview

Key Business KPIs

At the top of the dashboard, we start with the core KPIs, which provide an instant snapshot of business health. These figures update dynamically as filters such as Year, Region, and Organic Type are applied.

  • Total Transactions: 1,094
  • Revenue: £5.32M
  • Cost: £2.87M
  • Profit: £2.45M
  • Profit Margin: 46%

Profit & Revenue Trend Analysis

The monthly trend analysis shows a dip in February, followed by steady growth through June (peak performance). A slight decline appears in July–August, suggesting seasonality or shifting demand. This supports planning for promotions, staffing, and inventory during peak and off-peak periods.

Profit vs Boxes Shipped

This view highlights a strong positive relationship between shipment volume and profit. Higher boxes shipped translate into higher profitability—indicating operational efficiency as volume scales.

Channel Performance Analysis

Transactions by Channel

The dashboard compares transaction volume across channels such as Website, Warehouse, and Social Media, helping identify where customer demand is strongest.

Profit by Channel

The Website channel generates the highest profit contribution, while Social Media produces volume with comparatively lower profitability. This supports prioritising website promotions and optimising social media pricing/costs.

Product & Category Profitability

Category analysis shows Dark Chocolate as the most profitable segment, followed by Milk and Nut Chocolate. Product-level performance highlights top profit drivers such as Eclairs and 99% Dark & Pure. These insights support product mix optimisation and premium positioning.

Regional Sales Performance

The geographic view highlights major revenue contributors such as Italy, Spain, and Germany, while also identifying underperforming regions that may require targeted marketing, distribution review, or pricing adjustments.

Salesperson Performance

Salespeople are ranked by profit contribution against a €100,000 profit target. The Top/Bottom performer views enable leadership to recognise high performers and flag coaching or territory optimisation needs.

Organic vs Non-Organic Product Insights

Organic products perform strongly in premium regions and website sales, while non-organic products dominate high-volume warehouse activity. This supports a differentiated channel strategy, marketing focus, and future product development planning.

Dashboard Interactivity

All visuals are fully interactive. Users can filter by Year, Region, and Organic Type, and the dashboard updates in real time—enabling deeper exploration of performance drivers.

Conclusion

In summary, this Tableau dashboard provides Choco de Luxe with a comprehensive view of profitability and performance across products, channels, regions, and sales teams—empowering leaders to make strategic, data-driven decisions that support sustainable growth.
