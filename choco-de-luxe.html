Tableau Case Study

Sales & Regional Performance Analytics

Client: Choco de Luxe  |  Industry: Premium Food & Beverage  |  Tool: Tableau

An interactive Tableau solution designed to analyse profitability, regional performance, channel effectiveness, and salesforce productivity across European markets.

Swipe or click dots to explore dashboards

Business Problem

Despite strong brand equity and premium products, Choco de Luxe lacked a centralised, data-driven view of sales and operational performance across European regions.

  • Underperforming cities and regions
  • Limited visibility into channel profitability
  • Inconsistent delivery outcomes
  • Difficulty tracking salesperson productivity

Project Objective

To analyse sales performance across locations, channels, products, and salespeople, and deliver interactive Tableau dashboards that support strategic decisions on growth, pricing, and resource allocation.

Data Model

The analysis uses a star schema with a central sales fact table connected to product, location, and salesperson dimensions.

Tools & Methodology

  • Modelled fact and dimension tables for analytical reporting
  • Created calculated fields for revenue, cost, profit, and margins
  • Applied filters and parameters for dynamic analysis
  • Designed executive-level Tableau dashboards

Key KPIs Tracked

  • Total Transactions
  • Total Revenue, Cost, and Profit
  • Profit Margin (%)
  • Boxes Shipped
  • Salesperson Profit Contribution

Performance Insights

Trend analysis reveals seasonal demand patterns, with peak performance observed mid-year and slight declines during summer months. A strong positive relationship exists between shipment volume and profit, indicating efficient scaling of operations.

Channel & Product Analysis

Website sales deliver the highest profitability, while social media channels generate volume at lower margins. Dark Chocolate products lead profitability, supporting premium positioning and product mix optimisation.

Regional & Salesperson Performance

Italy, Spain, and Germany emerge as top-performing markets. Salesperson rankings against a €100,000 profit target enable recognition of high performers and identification of coaching opportunities.

Dashboard Interactivity

Users can filter by Year, Region, and Organic Product Type. All views update dynamically, enabling rapid drill-down into performance drivers.

Business Impact

  • Improved visibility into profitability across regions and channels
  • Data-driven product and pricing strategy support
  • Clear performance tracking for sales teams
  • Stronger foundation for regional expansion planning

Skills Demonstrated

Tableau • Profitability Analysis • Geographic Analytics • KPI Design • Sales Performance Tracking • Dashboard Storytelling
