Case Study

Café Harmony Dashboard

Industry: Food & Beverage / Hospitality  |  Tool: Microsoft Excel

Sales, operations, and customer insights dashboard built to support growth decisions across multiple locations.

Business Context

Café Harmony is a fast-growing virtual café chain. After a year of rapid growth, the business needed a data-driven way to manage scaling challenges such as inventory control, performance variability by location, and customer experience consistency.

Project Objective

Clean, analyze, and visualize Café Harmony’s business data in Excel, delivering a dashboard that supports strategic decision-making, operational efficiency, and sustainable growth.

Data Sources

  • Sales Data: Items sold, prices, quantities, locations, and dates
  • Customer Data: Age, gender, location, preferred menu items
  • Employee Data: Roles, salaries, and assigned locations
  • Stock Data: Inventory levels of ingredients and supplies
  • Feedback Data: Customer ratings and comments

Key Insights from the Dashboard

  • Sales Trend: Monthly performance patterns reveal seasonality and demand shifts.
  • Revenue by Product: Identifies top revenue items for promotions and menu optimization.
  • Employee Performance: Highlights top employees and allows filtering by role/location.
  • Stock Performance: Flags low/overstock items to reduce stockouts and waste.
  • Customer Ratings: Shows satisfaction distribution to guide service improvements.

Business Value

  • Improved visibility into performance across locations and roles.
  • Reduced operational risk by highlighting inventory issues early.
  • Supported targeted marketing and menu decisions using product insights.
  • Provided a single view for management to act faster with data.

Skills Demonstrated

Data Cleaning • Excel Functions (SUMIF/COUNTIF/VLOOKUP/INDEX-MATCH) • Pivot Tables • Slicers • KPI Design • Dashboard Storytelling
