Performance Case Study

Sales Performance & Customer Insights Analytics

Client: NovaMed Solutions  |  Industry: Pharmaceutical Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

An end-to-end Power BI solution designed to improve visibility into sales performance, profitability, product demand, and customer behaviour.

Tip: Swipe/trackpad left-right or click the dots to view the next dashboard.

Business Problem

NovaMed Solutions faced challenges related to demand forecasting, inventory planning, and limited visibility into product and customer performance. Although large volumes of sales data were available, stakeholders lacked a centralised analytics platform to support strategic decision-making.

Project Objective

  • Monitor sales, profit, and margin performance
  • Identify top- and underperforming drugs and customers
  • Understand customer demographics and purchasing patterns
  • Enable data-driven sales and inventory optimisation

Data Overview

  • Sales Data: transactions, revenue, quantities, time series
  • Drug Data: pricing, production cost, treatment categories
  • Customer Data: demographics, buyer type, geography

Methodology & Tools

  • Data cleaning and modelling using Power BI relationships
  • DAX measures for Revenue, Profit, COGS, Margin, Rankings
  • Dynamic Top/Bottom N analysis
  • Interactive visuals with slicers and drill-down

Key KPIs

  • Total Revenue
  • Total Profit & Profit Margin
  • Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
  • Quantity Sold

Dashboard Insights

Sales & Profitability

KPIs provide a real-time snapshot of business performance, while month-over-month analysis reveals growth trends and seasonal fluctuations.

Product Performance

Dynamic Top & Bottom analysis highlights drugs that drive the majority of revenue and those that negatively impact margins.

Customer Contribution

Customer ranking identifies high-value accounts and underperforming segments, supporting targeted engagement and contract optimisation.

Customer Demographics & Geography

Demographic and geographic analysis reveals revenue concentration by buyer type, age group, gender, and country — informing market expansion strategies.

Business Impact

  • Improved visibility into sales and profitability drivers
  • Better identification of high-value products and customers
  • Support for data-driven inventory and pricing decisions
  • Actionable insights for commercial and strategic teams

Skills Demonstrated

Power BI • Data Modelling • DAX • KPI Design • Ranking Logic • Customer Analytics • Data Storytelling
