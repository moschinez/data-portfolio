Power BI Case Study

Sales Performance & Customer Insights Dashboard

Client: NovaMed Solutions  |  Industry: Pharmaceutical Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

Project Overview

NovaMed Solutions is a pharmaceutical distributor serving a wide healthcare market. The company faced challenges with sales optimisation, inventory efficiency, and customer performance visibility. This project transformed raw sales and customer data into interactive Power BI dashboards to support data-driven decisions.

Business Objectives

  • Monitor overall sales and profitability performance
  • Identify top- and underperforming drugs and customers
  • Analyse customer demographics and purchasing behaviour
  • Uncover geographic and market-based revenue trends

Data Summary

  • Drug Data: pricing, production cost, regulatory compliance, treatment category
  • Sales Data: transaction-level sales, units sold, revenue, buyer type, time trends
  • Customer Data: demographics, customer type, and geographic location

Tools & Techniques

  • Power BI (data modelling, relationships, interactive visuals)
  • DAX Measures: Revenue, Profit, COGS, Profit Margin
  • Dynamic Ranking: Top/Bottom performers
  • Slicers for drill-down by date, customer, drug, buyer type, and country

Dashboard Deliverables

Dashboard 1: Top & Bottom Sales Analysis

  • KPIs: Revenue, Profit, COGS, Quantity Sold, Profit Margin
  • Month-over-Month comparisons for Revenue and Profit
  • Top 5 / Bottom 5 drugs by Revenue, Profit, and Sales Volume
  • Customer performance ranking with contribution analysis

Dashboard 2: Customer Analysis

  • Customer demographics by age, gender, and buyer type
  • Average revenue per customer segment
  • Revenue breakdown by country and customer type
  • Geographic revenue insights (map/heatmap, top markets highlighted)

Impact

  • Improved visibility into profitability drivers and sales trends
  • Flagged underperforming products/customers for optimisation
  • Revealed high-value segments and strongest geographic markets
  • Supported better planning for inventory and customer engagement
