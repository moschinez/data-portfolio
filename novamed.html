Power BI Case Study

Sales Performance & Customer Insights Dashboard

Client: NovaMed Solutions  |  Industry: Pharmaceutical Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

Swipe/trackpad or click dots to view the next dashboard

Introduction

This Power BI solution was designed for NovaMed Solutions, a pharmaceutical distributor. The dashboard provides visibility into sales performance, profitability, and customer behaviour to support data-driven decision-making.

Dashboard 1: Top & Bottom Sales Analysis

Overall Sales Performance

Key KPIs include Total Revenue, Total Profit, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), Quantity Sold, and Profit Margin, offering an instant snapshot of business performance.

Month-over-Month Performance

Revenue and profit trends are compared month-over-month to identify growth patterns, seasonality, and performance declines.

Top & Bottom Performing Drugs

The dashboard dynamically ranks the Top 5 and Bottom 5 drugs by revenue, profit, and sales volume, including contribution percentages.

Customer Performance Analysis

Customer contribution analysis highlights high-value customers and identifies underperforming accounts.

Interactivity

All visuals respond dynamically to slicers, allowing users to drill down by time period, product, customer, and buyer type.

Dashboard 2: Customer Analysis

Customer Demographics

Customers are segmented by age group, gender, and buyer type to better understand purchasing behaviour.

Revenue per Customer Segment

Average revenue per customer segment reveals the most valuable customer groups.

Revenue Distribution

Revenue is analysed by country and buyer type to highlight key geographic markets.

Geographic Insights

Map visuals highlight top revenue-generating countries and support market expansion decisions.

Conclusion

  • Clear visibility into sales and profitability
  • Identification of high- and low-performing drugs and customers
  • Improved understanding of customer and regional trends
