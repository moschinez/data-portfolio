Power BI Case Study

Sales Performance & Customer Insights Analytics

Client: NovaMed Solutions  |  Industry: Pharmaceutical Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

Tip: Swipe/trackpad left-right or click the dots to view the next dashboard.

Problem Statement

NovaMed Solutions, a pharmaceutical distributor serving a broad healthcare market, faced operational challenges driven by:

  • Inefficient demand forecasting and inventory management
  • Limited visibility into sales performance and profitability
  • Lack of actionable insights into customer behaviour and market opportunities

Despite collecting detailed sales and customer data, the organisation needed a centralised, analytical solution to turn raw data into business intelligence.

Project Objective

Design interactive Power BI dashboards that enable stakeholders to:

  • Monitor key sales and profitability metrics
  • Identify top- and underperforming drugs and customers
  • Understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior
  • Support data-driven decision-making and operational efficiency

Data Overview

The analysis was based on three core datasets:

  • Drug Data: pricing, production cost, regulatory compliance, and treatment categories
  • Sales Data: transaction-level details including units sold, revenue, buyer type, and time series data
  • Customer Data: demographics, geographic location, and customer segmentation

Approach & Methodology

  • Cleaned and modelled relational datasets in Power BI
  • Created calculated measures using DAX for revenue, profit, COGS, and margins
  • Implemented dynamic ranking logic for Top/Bottom analysis
  • Designed interactive visuals with slicers for flexible exploration

Introduction

Today, I’ll be walking you through an interactive Power BI dashboard designed for NovaMed Solutions, a pharmaceutical distributor. The goal of this dashboard is to provide clear visibility into sales performance, profitability, and customer behaviour, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Dashboard 1: Top & Bottom Sales Analysis

Overall Sales Performance

Let’s start with the top-level KPIs. At the top of the dashboard, we can see key performance indicators including Total Revenue, Total Profit, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), Quantity Sold, and Profit Margin.

These KPIs provide an instant snapshot of business health and update dynamically based on filters such as date, drug, or customer.

Month-over-Month Performance

This visual compares month-over-month revenue and profit. This allows stakeholders to quickly identify growth patterns, seasonal trends, or performance drops that may require intervention.

Top & Bottom Performing Drugs

The dashboard includes a dynamic ranking of the Top 5 and Bottom 5 drugs based on revenue, profit, or sales volume. Each ranking also shows percentage contribution, making it easy to identify which products drive the majority of sales and which may be underperforming or affecting margins.

Customer Performance Analysis

This section focuses on customer contribution. It highlights top-performing customers as well as those generating minimal revenue. These insights support customer engagement improvements, contract review, and prioritisation of high-value accounts.

Interactivity

All visuals are fully interactive. By selecting a specific drug, customer, or time period using slicers, the entire dashboard updates in real time, allowing users to drill down into performance drivers.

Dashboard 2: Customer Analysis

Customer Demographics Overview

The Customer Analysis dashboard begins with customer demographics. It breaks customers down by age group, gender, and buyer type to reveal who the core customers are.

Revenue per Customer Segment

This view analyses average revenue per customer segment, helping identify which groups (for example, hospitals versus individual buyers) generate the highest value.

Revenue Distribution

This visual shows revenue distribution by buyer type and country, highlighting how different customer categories and regions contribute to sales.

Purchasing Trends by Demographics

This section examines revenue trends by age and gender, supporting targeted marketing and product positioning.

Geographic Insights

The map highlights geographic revenue concentration and emphasises the top revenue-generating countries, helping leadership identify key markets and potential expansion opportunities.

Conclusion

In summary, these dashboards provide NovaMed Solutions with:

  • Clear visibility into sales and profitability
  • Identification of high- and low-performing drugs and customers
  • Deeper understanding of customer demographics and regional performance

Overall, the solution empowers stakeholders to make strategic, data-driven decisions to optimise sales, inventory, and customer engagement.
