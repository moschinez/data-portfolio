Tableau Dashboard Walkthrough

Choco de Luxe

Industry: Premium Food & Beverage  |  Tool: Tableau

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyse sales performance, regional trends, channel profitability, product margins, and salesperson productivity across Europe.

Introduction

Today, I’ll be walking you through an interactive Tableau dashboard built for Choco de Luxe, a premium artisanal chocolate brand operating across multiple European cities. The dashboard provides a unified view of sales performance, regional trends, channel profitability, product margins, and salesperson productivity.

Executive Sales Performance Overview

The dashboard opens with key performance indicators, including total transactions, revenue, cost, profit, and profit margin. These KPIs update dynamically based on filters such as Year, Region, and Organic Type.

  • Transactions: 1,094
  • Revenue: £5.32M
  • Cost: £2.87M
  • Profit: £2.45M
  • Profit Margin: 46%

Profit & Revenue Trend Analysis

Monthly trends from January through August show a dip in February, steady growth through June, and a slight decline in July and August, suggesting seasonality in demand.

Channel Performance Analysis

The Website channel generates the highest profit contribution, while Social Media produces transaction volume with lower margins. This insight supports channel prioritisation and pricing optimisation.

Product & Category Profitability

Dark Chocolate is the top-performing category by profit. Products such as Eclairs and 99% Dark & Pure drive the highest margins, reinforcing the premium product strategy.

Regional Sales Performance

The geographic map highlights Italy, Spain, and Germany as top revenue contributors, while identifying underperforming regions for targeted intervention.

Salesperson Performance

Salespeople are ranked against a €100,000 profit benchmark, allowing leadership to identify top performers for recognition and lower performers for coaching or territory review.

Organic vs Non-Organic Products

Organic products perform strongly via website sales and premium regions, while non-organic products dominate high-volume warehouse transactions.

Dashboard Preview

Conclusion

This Tableau dashboard delivers a clear, executive-level view of Choco de Luxe’s sales and profitability across products, channels, regions, and sales teams—enabling data-driven decisions that support sustainable growth.
