HR Analytics Case Study

HR Analytics & Workforce Insights Analytics

Client: NextGen Corp  |  Industry: Technology (Software & Hardware)
Tools: PostgreSQL, SQL

Project Overview

NextGen Corp is a fast-growing technology company focused on innovation in both software and hardware. As the organisation scaled, leadership identified concerns around employee turnover, performance variability, and salary disparities across departments.

Business Problem

The HR department needed a data-driven solution to understand workforce trends, improve retention, evaluate performance consistency, and ensure fair compensation across departments.

Project Objectives

  • Identify employee retention and turnover trends
  • Evaluate performance across departments
  • Analyze salary distribution and fairness
  • Support HR decision-making with SQL-driven insights

Data & Tools

Analysis was conducted using a relational PostgreSQL database containing:

  • Employees, Departments, Performance, Attendance
  • Turnover records and exit reasons
  • Historical salary data

Key Analysis

Employee Retention Analysis

  • Identified top 5 longest-serving employees
  • Calculated department-level turnover rates
  • Flagged employees at risk based on performance and attendance
  • Analysed primary reasons for employee exits

Performance Analysis

  • Segmented employees by high (5.0) and low (<3.5) performance
  • Identified departments with performance gaps
  • Calculated average performance score by department

Salary Analysis

  • Computed total salary expense
  • Analysed average salary by job title
  • Identified employees earning above $80,000
  • Evaluated salary-to-performance correlation

Business Impact

  • Highlighted departments with high turnover risk
  • Revealed performance inconsistencies requiring intervention
  • Identified compensation gaps impacting employee satisfaction
  • Enabled data-driven HR and retention strategies
