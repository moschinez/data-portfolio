Excel Case Study

Optimizing Order Fulfilment

Client: SwiftRoute Logistics  |  Industry: Logistics  |  Tool: Microsoft Excel

An interactive dashboard designed to provide visibility into delivery performance backlog, customer satisfaction and operational visibility.

Business Context

SwiftRoute Logistics Ltd., a UK-based last-mile delivery provider, faced growing operational challenges, including order backlogs, inconsistent delivery performance, and low customer satisfaction due to a lack of centralised tracking and analytics. In this and customer experience consistency.

I built a comprehensive Excel dashboard to analyse operational performance, uncover bottlenecks, and provide actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

Business Problem

SwiftRoute’s traditional reporting methods failed to highlight the root causes of delays, route inefficiencies, and customer dissatisfaction. Management needed a solution to: - Monitor delivery performance and order progress - Identify delivery bottlenecks and backlog drivers - Track customer feedback quantitatively - Provide operational visibility across routes and zones This project builds an interactive dashboard that consolidates key performance indicators and enables stakeholders to analyse performance from multiple perspectives.

Data Sources

  • Sales Data: Sales_ID, Customer_ID, Product, Quantity, Price_Per_Item, Location, Sale_Date and Total_Sale
  • Customer Data: Customer_ID, Age, Gender, and Preferred_items
  • Employee Data: Employee_ID, Name, Role, Location, Sales_Target, Sales_Achieved, and Performance
  • Stock Data: Stock_Items, Current_Stock, Ideal_Stock and Stock_Performance
  • Feedback Data: Feedback_ID, Customer_ID, Sentiment, Comment and Rating

Methodology & Tools

Data Cleaning

Removed duplicates, handled missing values, and standardised data formats to ensure consistency and accuracy across all datasets.

Data Analysis

Used Excel functions such as XLOOKUP, alongside Pivot Tables for aggregation, segmentation, and trend analysis.

Dashboard Design

Designed an interactive Excel dashboard using KPIs, charts, pivot visuals, and slicers to enable flexible exploration and insight-driven decision-making.

Introduction

Today, I’ll be walking you through an interactive Excel dashboard built for Café Harmony, a fast-growing virtual café chain. The goal of this dashboard is to provide clear visibility into sales performance, customer behaviour, employee productivity, inventory levels, and customer satisfaction, enabling data-driven operational and growth decisions.

Sales Performance Overview

Key Business KPIs

Let’s begin with the key performance indicators displayed on the left side of the dashboard. These include Sales Count, Total Sales, Employee Count, and Average Customer Rating.

These KPIs provide an immediate snapshot of overall business performance and update dynamically as filters such as location, role, or stock level are applied.

Sales Trend Analysis

The Sales Trend chart displays monthly sales performance from January through December. This visual helps identify seasonal demand patterns, periods of growth and decline, and months that may require promotional activity or operational adjustment.

Customer & Revenue Insights

Gender Distribution

This chart shows the distribution of customers by gender. Female customers contribute slightly more to overall sales than male customers, offering insight into the café’s core customer base.

Revenue by Product

The Revenue by Product chart highlights which menu items generate the most revenue. Items such as Muffins, Cappuccino, and Iced Tea emerge as top performers, supporting menu optimisation and promotional planning.

Employee Performance Analysis

The Top Employees section ranks employees based on their sales contribution. This allows management to identify high-performing staff for recognition and flag underperformance that may require additional training or support.

Role and location filters enable deeper analysis of employee performance across different café branches.

Stock Performance & Inventory Management

This section highlights inventory movement across key ingredients and supplies. Items such as Milk, Croissant Dough, and Iced Tea Bags frequently run low, indicating potential stockout risks.

These insights support improved inventory planning, reduced waste, and smoother daily operations.

Customer Feedback Analysis

The Customer Ratings chart displays the distribution of customer satisfaction scores on a scale from 1 to 5. Most ratings fall between 3 and 5, indicating generally positive customer experiences while still highlighting opportunities for improvement.

Dashboard Interactivity

All visuals in this dashboard are fully interactive. Users can filter the data by role, location, and current stock levels. As selections are made, the entire dashboard updates in real time, allowing deeper exploration of performance drivers.

Conclusion

In summary, this dashboard provides Café Harmony with clear visibility into sales trends, customer behaviour, employee performance, inventory health, and customer satisfaction.

Overall, the solution empowers management to make strategic, data-driven decisions that improve operational efficiency, enhance customer experience, and support sustainable business growth.
