Excel Case Study

Optimizing Order Fulfillment

Client: SwiftRoute Logistics Ltd.  |  Industry: Logistics & Supply Chain  |  Tool: Microsoft Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to improve delivery performance, reduce order backlogs, and enhance operational visibility across routes and zones.

Business Context

SwiftRoute Logistics Ltd. is a Manchester-based logistics company with over twenty years of experience providing nationwide transportation and last-mile delivery services across the United Kingdom.

As order volumes increased, the company began experiencing growing order backlogs, inconsistent delivery times, and rising customer complaints. The absence of centralised performance tracking made it difficult for management to identify bottlenecks and proactively address delays.

Project Objective

To analyse order fulfilment data using Microsoft Excel and deliver an interactive dashboard that tracks delivery performance, identifies the root causes of delays and backlogs, and supports data-driven operational decisions.

Data Sources

  • Order Data: Order ID, Route, Delivery Zone, Order Status
  • Delivery Metrics: Delivery Time, Delay Duration
  • Operations: Driver ID, Vehicle Type, Allocation Rules
  • Customer Feedback: Positive, Neutral, Negative, No Feedback

Methodology & Tools

Data Structuring

Cleaned and standardised route and zone labels, created derived fields such as delayed orders and backlog flags, and structured the dataset into Excel Tables for reliable aggregation.

Data Analysis

Used Pivot Tables and calculated fields to compute KPIs, including backlog rate, CSAT percentage, delay rate, and average delivery time across routes and zones.

Dashboard Design

Built a fully interactive Excel dashboard using KPI tiles, charts, slicers, and conditional formatting to enable dynamic performance analysis.

Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard tracks four core KPIs:

  • Total Orders
  • Average Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
  • Backlog Rate
  • Total Backlog Volume

These KPIs update dynamically as filters are applied, providing real-time operational visibility.

Route & Zone Performance Analysis

Analysis by route shows that Routes 1 and 2 have the highest backlog rates (above 51%) and among the lowest CSAT scores, indicating systemic delivery challenges rather than isolated incidents.

Route 5 presents an interesting pattern — while it has one of the lowest average delays, its CSAT remains low, suggesting frequent minor delays or unmet delivery expectations.

Backlog & Delay Insights

Zone-level analysis reveals that Zone 2 has the highest delay percentage and backlog rate, making it a priority area for routing optimisation and resource reallocation.

Customer Feedback Analysis

Customer feedback analysis shows that only about 15% of deliveries receive positive feedback, while over half of the orders have no feedback recorded. This highlights both service improvement opportunities and gaps in customer engagement.

Dashboard Interactivity

The dashboard includes slicers for delivery zone, route, driver, vehicle type, order status, and customer feedback. All visuals update instantly, allowing stakeholders to drill down into specific operational segments.

Expected Business Impact

  • 20–30% reduction in order backlogs through improved routing
  • More consistent delivery times and higher on-time delivery rates
  • Reduced operational costs from fewer expedited deliveries
  • Improved customer satisfaction through targeted interventions
  • Centralized, automated performance reporting
