Excel Case Study

Business Intelligence Analytics

Client: Café Harmony  |  Industry: Food & Beverage / Hospitality  |  Tool: Microsoft Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard built to improve operational visibility across sales performance, customer behaviour, employee productivity, inventory management, and customer satisfaction.

Business Context

Café Harmony is a fast-growing virtual café chain operating across multiple city locations. Following a period of rapid expansion, the business began facing challenges around inventory control, sales visibility, employee performance tracking, and consistency in customer experience.

Management required a centralised, data-driven solution to monitor performance and support informed operational and strategic decision-making.

Project Objective

To clean, analyse, and visualise Café Harmony’s operational data in Excel, delivering an interactive dashboard that supports performance monitoring, operational efficiency, and sustainable business growth.

Data Sources

  • Sales Data: Sales ID, Product, Quantity, Price, Location, Sale Date, Total Sales
  • Customer Data: Customer ID, Age, Gender, Preferred Items
  • Employee Data: Employee ID, Role, Location, Sales Target vs Achieved
  • Stock Data: Item Name, Current Stock, Ideal Stock, Stock Status
  • Feedback Data: Rating, Sentiment, Customer Comments

Methodology & Tools

Data Preparation

Removed duplicates, handled missing values, standardised data formats, and structured datasets using Excel Tables to ensure consistency and accuracy.

Analysis Techniques

Leveraged Excel functions such as XLOOKUP, alongside Pivot Tables and calculated fields for aggregation, segmentation, and trend analysis.

Dashboard Design

Designed an interactive dashboard using KPIs, charts, pivot visuals, conditional formatting, and slicers to enable dynamic exploration and insight-driven decision-making.

Key KPIs Tracked

  • Total Sales & Sales Volume
  • Average Customer Rating
  • Employee Sales Performance
  • Inventory Stock Health

Sales & Revenue Insights

Monthly sales trend analysis reveals seasonal demand patterns, supporting better forecasting and promotional planning. Revenue by product highlights top-performing menu items such as Muffins, Cappuccino, and Iced Tea.

Employee Performance Analysis

Employees are ranked based on sales contribution, enabling management to recognise high performers and identify training opportunities for underperforming staff.

Inventory & Stock Management

Stock performance analysis highlights frequently depleted items, helping reduce stockout risks, minimise waste, and improve day-to-day operational planning.

Customer Feedback Analysis

Customer ratings and sentiment analysis provide insight into the overall satisfaction levels, highlighting strengths while identifying areas requiring service improvement.

Dashboard Interactivity

The dashboard includes slicers for location, role, and stock status. All visuals update dynamically, allowing stakeholders to drill down into specific business segments in real time.

Business Impact

  • Improved visibility into sales, inventory, and employee performance
  • Reduced risk of stockouts through proactive inventory tracking
  • Better workforce performance monitoring and accountability
  • Data-driven insights to support operational and growth decisions

Skills Demonstrated

Data Cleaning • Excel Functions (XLOOKUP) • Pivot Tables • KPI Design • Slicers • Dashboard Design • Business Analytics & Storytelling
