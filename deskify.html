Performance Case Study

Product & Profitability Performance Dashboard

Client: Deskify Office Supplies  |  Industry: Retail & Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

An interactive Power BI solution designed to provide executive visibility into revenue, profit drivers, customer segments, and regional performance.

Tip: Swipe or click dots to explore dashboards.

Business Problem

Deskify Office Supplies required a centralised performance view across products, customers, and regions to support profitability-focused decision-making.

  • Limited visibility into product-level profit contribution
  • Difficulty identifying high-value customer segments
  • Regional performance disparities lacked clarity
  • No executive-ready reporting for strategic planning

Project Objective

Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to:

  • Track revenue, profit, orders, customers, and margins
  • Analyze category and sub-category profitability
  • Identify top customers and regions driving profit
  • Enable data-driven strategic and operational decisions

Data Overview

  • Orders: Sales, profit, quantity, shipping mode, order dates
  • Products: Category, sub-category, product names
  • Customers: Customer segments and profitability
  • Geography: Region- and state-level performance

Analytical Approach

Data was modelled and analysed using Power BI with calculated measures, relationships, and interactive visuals to support drill-down analysis across time, products, customers, and regions.

Dashboard Highlights

Executive KPI Overview

The dashboard opens with KPIs, including Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders, Customer Count, and Profit Margin, offering an immediate snapshot of business health.

Product & Sub-Category Profitability

Visuals highlight profit contribution across Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture, with sub-category analysis revealing top and underperforming products.

Customer & Regional Performance

Tree maps and bar visuals identify high-value customers and regions that drive overall profitability.

Time-Series Trend Analysis

Monthly profit trends reveal seasonality, peaks, and downturns, supporting forecasting and promotional planning.

Business Impact

  • Clear visibility into profit drivers and loss-making areas
  • Improved customer and regional segmentation
  • Actionable insights to support pricing and product strategy
  • Executive-ready reporting for faster decision-making

Skills Demonstrated

Power BI • Data Modelling • DAX Measures • KPI Design • Interactive Dashboards • Business Analytics & Storytelling
