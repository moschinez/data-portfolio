Performance Case Study

Product & Profitability Performance Dashboard

Client: Deskify Office Supplies  |  Industry: Retail & Distribution  |  Tool: Power BI

Tip: Swipe or click dots to explore dashboards.

Problem Statement

Deskify Office Supplies required a consolidated view of product, customer, and regional performance across multiple years to support profitability-driven decision-making.

  • Limited visibility into high- and low-performing product categories
  • Difficulty identifying profitable customer segments
  • Regional performance disparities not clearly understood
  • Need for executive-ready performance reporting

Project Objective

Develop interactive Tableau dashboards to:

  • Track revenue, profit, orders, and customer growth
  • Analyze product and sub-category profitability
  • Identify top-performing customers and regions
  • Enable data-driven strategic planning

Data Overview

  • Orders Data: sales, profit, quantity, ship mode, and time series
  • Product Data: categories, sub-categories, and product names
  • Customer Data: customer segments and profitability
  • Geographic Data: regional and state-level performance

Dashboard Walkthrough

Executive KPI Overview

The dashboard opens with key KPIs including Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders, Customer Count, and Profit Margin. These metrics provide an immediate snapshot of business performance.

Product Category Profitability

Donut charts and bar visuals highlight profit contribution by Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture, clearly showing Office Supplies as the dominant profit driver.

Sub-Category Analysis

A ranked bar chart reveals top-performing sub-categories such as Binders, Paper, and Furnishings, while also identifying underperforming items like Copiers.

Customer & Regional Insights

Tree maps and flow visuals analyse profit by customer name, segment, and region, allowing stakeholders to pinpoint high-value customers and regions driving overall profitability.

Time-Series Profit Trends

Monthly profit trends highlight seasonality, peaks, and declines, supporting forecasting and promotional planning.

Conclusion

This Tableau solution provides Deskify with a comprehensive, interactive view of performance across products, customers, and regions.

  • Improved visibility into profit drivers
  • Clear identification of growth opportunities
  • Actionable insights for strategic decision-making
